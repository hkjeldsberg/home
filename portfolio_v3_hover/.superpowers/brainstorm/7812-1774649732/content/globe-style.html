What style of globe do you want in the hero?

This is the centrepiece of the homepage — the treatment shapes the whole feel of the site.

Dot-Matrix Globe

World map rendered as animated dots — rotating slowly. Similar to GitHub's globe. Minimal, technical, elegant. Uses cobe or globe.gl library.

Wireframe / Mesh Globe

Clean latitude/longitude grid lines, glowing accent colour. More abstract and architectural. Strong for a research/engineering identity.

Arc-Connection Globe

Glowing arcs connecting cities/regions — communicates global reach and collaboration. Great for someone working across disciplines and geographies. Used on many high-end agency sites.
