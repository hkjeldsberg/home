Which accent colour for the dark theme?

ELECTRIC BLUE · #3B82F6

Technical, trustworthy, classic for engineering portfolios. Pairs naturally with the wireframe globe aesthetic.

Distinctive and memorable. Used by Vercel, Linear, and many dark-mode SaaS products. Evokes growth and precision.

CRIMSON · #E63946

Your current accent from v3. Bold and decisive on a dark background — strong brand continuity across versions.
